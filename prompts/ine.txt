This is a Mexican INE (Instituto Nacional Electoral) voter ID card.

Extract:
- nombre_completo: Full name (usually APELLIDO_PATERNO APELLIDO_MATERNO NOMBRE(S))
- domicilio: Complete address as shown
- fecha_nacimiento: Date of birth in DD/MM/YYYY format
- clave_elector: Voter key (alphanumeric, 18 chars)
- curp: CURP if visible (18 chars, may be on the back)
- seccion: Electoral section number
- vigencia: Expiration year

Note: INE cards have front and back. The front has the photo, name, and address.
The back has the CURP, voter key, and barcode.
